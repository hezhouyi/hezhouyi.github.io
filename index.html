About me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Chemistry, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology supervised by Prof. Haibin SU. Currently, I work on theoretical Chemistry and computational Biology. My research interests especially lie on statistical mechanics and CRISPR Cas9 systems.

I proudly received my B.Sc. degree in Chemical Physics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), which is considered one of the best Chinese Universities in the fields of frontier research and undergraduate education. During my undergraduate years, I was a visiting student at HKUST for 2 months. Publication related to my undergraduate thesis is now available Unit Cell Consistency of Maximally Localized Wannier Functions.

I am interested in interdisciplinary research and open to all kinds of research-like jobs.
